Join the Force March Madness Bracket Challenge!

Thank you for your interest in joining our March Madness Bracket Challenge! We're thrilled to have you participate in this exciting event, where competition meets charity. Below, you'll find all the details you need to get started, from signing up to making your picks, and, of course, the charitable aspect of our challenge if you're into that sort of thing.

Step 1: Registration and Bracket Creation

First, if you haven't already, please create your bracket on Yahoo! Fantasy by following this link: Yahoo! Fantasy League. Make sure to note down your bracket name or ID as you'll need it for the registration form.

Step 2: Filling Out the Registration Form

Once you have your bracket set up, please fill out our registration form here: Insert Registration Form Link. In this form, you'll need to provide your name, email, Yahoo! Fantasy bracket name/ID, and confirm the number of brackets you're entering. This is crucial for ensuring your entry is correctly recorded and matched with your payment.

Step 3: Payment

The entry fee is $50 for one bracket or $100 for three brackets. Please send your payment via [Preferred Payment Platform] to [Your Payment Information]. In the payment description or note, kindly include "March Madness - [Your Email] - [Bracket IDs]" to help us match your payment to your bracket(s) seamlessly.

Step 4: Confirmation

After we receive both your completed registration form and payment, we'll send you a confirmation email. This email will confirm your participation, payment, and provide you with any additional information needed as we approach the tournament start.

Our Charity Goal

This year, we're excited to announce that 60% of all proceeds will be donated to DeAnza Force, supporting the 2010G ECNL team. Not only does your participation offer a chance to win great prizes, but it also contributes to a fantastic cause.

Prize Pool Distribution

We are committed to a rewarding experience for our participants while supporting our chosen charitable cause. The prize pool will be allocated as follows:

  • 1st Place: 50% of the prize pool. The champion takes home half the pot for their superior bracket predictions and an all-encompassing smugness that will last you all year long.
  • 2nd Place: 30% of the prize pool. A deserving prize for the runner-up in our competition and a deservingly smaller amount for the first loser.
  • 3rd Place: 10% of the prize pool. Recognition and reward for making it to the top three and bragging rights ... I guess?
  • Best Upset Pick: 10% of the prize pool. This special category rewards the participant who successfully predicts the upset with the maximum seed differential. In the event of a tie, the next biggest upset pick will serve as the tiebreaker. Obviously, this is the true winner of the event. Anyone can ape the seeded rankings, picking an upset take true panache.

Thank you for supporting our team. We're looking forward to a fantastic tournament and can't wait to see your picks!
